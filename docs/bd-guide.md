## 1. Getting Started

### First Login

You will receive a **welcome email** with:
- Your **username** and **temporary password**
- The **reseller** you belong to
- A link to the **login page**: `/my/login/`

> **Important:** Change your password after first login via **Profile → Change Password**.

### Forgot Password

1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your username or email
3. Check email for a **6-digit reset code** (valid 15 minutes)
4. Enter the code + new password

## 3. Your QR Code

Go to **QR Code** from the sidebar. You have **one QR code**.

### How it works

```
Customer scans your QR
  → If 1 product assigned → checkout directly
  → If multiple products → customer chooses which product
  → Customer pays normally
  → Order linked to you → commission recorded
```

The customer sees a **normal checkout** — no referral info visible.

### Actions

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Copy Link** | Copy URL to clipboard — share via WhatsApp, Telegram |
| **Download PNG** | Save as image (600x600) — print for name cards, brochures |
| **Share** | Native share on mobile |

### Assigned Products

Below your QR code, your assigned products are shown as chips (e.g., `A01`, `Series 1`). These are inherited from your reseller.

> **No QR code?** Your admin hasn't assigned products yet. Contact them.

## 5. Your Profile

Go to **Profile** from the sidebar.

| Section | Fields |
|---------|--------|

1. Scroll to **Change Password**

## 6. FAQ

**Q: I forgot my password.**
A: Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page. You'll receive a 6-digit code by email.

**Q: My QR code is not showing.**
A: Your admin needs to assign products to your reseller first.

**Q: Can other BDs see my orders?**
A: No. You only see your own data.

**Q: What if a customer scans my QR and there are multiple products?**
A: They see a selection page to choose which product to buy. The sale is still attributed to you.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Commissions are reviewed by admin and paid to your bank account. Keep your bank details up to date in Profile.

**Q: My account is disabled.**
A: Contact your reseller manager or admin to reactivate.

**Q: Can I change my tracking code?**
A: No. Your tracking code is permanent and linked to your sales history.

**Q: The QR link works the same as scanning?**
A: Yes. Sharing the link via messaging apps works exactly the same as scanning the QR image.
